About That's Not My Neighbor

That's Not My Neighbor is a captivating game that has taken the itch.io platform by storm, offering a unique blend of horror and bureaucracy. Set in 1955, the game places you in the role of a doorman tasked with a chilling objective: to identify and prevent doppelgangers from infiltrating your apartment building.

Game Author

Created by solo game developer Nacho Sama, That's Not My Neighbor showcases an active presence in the independent game development community. Nacho Sama has also developed other intriguing titles, including "Unlikely," "Sofia?" and "Flesh, Blood, & Concrete," further establishing a reputation for crafting engaging and thought-provoking games.

Gameplay

In this unsettling world, you face the constant threat of doppelgangers—creatures capable of mimicking humans. Your job is to utilize various methods, from checking IDs to scrutinizing details, to discern the real tenants from their impersonators. However, these doppelgangers are clever, and the line between real and fake can blur, creating an atmosphere of tension and suspense.

That's Not My Neighbor combines the thrill of detective work with the unnerving vibe of a horror film. The pixelated art style adds a touch of charm while effectively enhancing the sense of dread. As you delve deeper into the game, you’ll encounter unsettling twists and turns, forcing you to question everything you thought you knew about your "neighbors."

Game Objects

Neighbors

In That's Not My Neighbor, the term "neighbor" takes on a deceptive meaning. While you interact with various tenants in the apartment building, these individuals are not necessarily who they seem. The core gameplay revolves around identifying and stopping doppelgangers who attempt to impersonate the real tenants and gain entry into the building. These doppelgangers can perfectly mimic the appearance and mannerisms of the actual residents, making it incredibly challenging to distinguish them.

Doppelgängers in That's Not My Neighbor

The doppelgängers in the game are shifty entities that will try their best to blend in with their targets. However, their perfect disguise often falters under close scrutiny. Here’s a breakdown of the key points about doppelgängers:

  • Appearance: They look exactly like the person they are impersonating.
  • Origin: Not related to the person they mimic.
  • Nature: In That's Not My Neighbor, they are portrayed as creatures, not supernatural beings.
  • Function: In the game, they attempt to enter the apartment building while disguised as real tenants.
